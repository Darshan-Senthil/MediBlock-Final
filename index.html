Features

BLOCK CHAIN

A blockchain, originally block chain, is a growing list of records, called blocks, which are linked using cryptography. Each block contains a cryptographic hash of the previous block, a timestamp, and transaction data . By design, a blockchain is resistant to modification of the data. It is "an open, distributed ledger that can record transactions between two parties efficiently and in a verifiable and permanent way".

About Us


Our prototype/model is going to track the medicine product from manufactured entities to the end-consumer.
We track the product by giving access to public blockchain (i.e anyone around the world can access) which gets updated time to time
whenever manufacturing entities update the blockchain about the product.There is one public blockchain maintained solely for the public
and one private blockchain for each of the pharmaceutical companies to add or update or modify
(if anything goes wrong) the detailed information about the medicine product.
